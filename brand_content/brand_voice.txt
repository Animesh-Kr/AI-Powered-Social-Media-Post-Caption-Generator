Our Brand Voice Guidelines
==========================

## Core Values
- Innovation-driven and forward-thinking
- Professional yet approachable
- Data-driven decision making
- Customer success focused
- Authentic and transparent communication

## Tone & Style

### Professional Tone
- Use active voice for clarity
- Keep sentences concise (15-20 words max)
- Include data/metrics when relevant
- End with clear call-to-action
- Avoid jargon unless industry-specific

### Approachable Style
- Use conversational language
- Include relevant emojis (2-3 per post)
- Ask engaging questions
- Share behind-the-scenes insights
- Celebrate team and customer wins

## Platform-Specific Guidelines

### LinkedIn
- Professional and thought-leadership focused
- Include industry insights and data
- Tag relevant people and companies
- Use 3-5 hashtags maximum
- Longer captions OK (up to 1300 characters)

### Instagram
- Visual storytelling emphasis
- Casual and engaging tone
- Use 10-15 hashtags
- Shorter captions (125-150 characters ideal)
- Include emoji throughout

### Twitter
- Concise and punchy (280 characters max)
- Use 1-2 hashtags
- Include relevant mentions
- Thread longer stories
- Timely and trending topics

## Content Themes
1. Product announcements and updates
2. Customer success stories
3. Industry insights and trends
4. Team culture and behind-the-scenes
5. Educational content and tips
6. Community engagement and events

## Do's and Don'ts

### Do:
✅ Start with attention-grabbing hooks
✅ Use specific numbers and metrics
✅ Include clear value propositions
✅ Tag relevant accounts
✅ Respond to comments promptly

### Don't:
❌ Use all caps (except for emphasis)
❌ Over-promise or use clickbait
❌ Ignore negative feedback
❌ Post without proofreading
❌ Use too many hashtags on LinkedIn
